Frequently asked questions
How does the streak work?⌄

Complete at least one concentration session on a given day and your streak goes up by one. Miss a whole day and it resets, but your longest streak is always kept.

Can I change how long breaks are?⌄

Yes. Select a tab, then use the − and + buttons under the timer to adjust that mode's length in 5 minute steps.

Where does the lofi music come from?⌄

It's generated live in your browser using simple audio synthesis, so there's nothing to download and no copyright to worry about.

What does pop out do?⌄

It opens a small separate window with just the countdown, so you can keep an eye on your session while using other apps.

Will my settings be saved?⌄

Your streak, durations and preferences are saved automatically. A custom photo background only lasts for the current session.
